Welcome to Vietnamese Language and Cultural Heritage School!

We are a Non-profit Organization in Snohomish County, gather more than thirty teachers and teacher's assistants who devote their times every Sunday to assist and inspirit kid to learn their second native language. We help children to understand the culture of 4000 years that they inherited from their parents and ancestors by celebrating every traditional holidays during the school years. Beside learning Vietnamese, our kids will also being taught how to be a good element of the society and to give back to the community.

The mission of the Lac Hong Vietnamese Language School is to preserve the Vietnamese heritage by educating students in Vietnamese language, culture, values, and history. The primary objectives of Lac Hong Vietnamese Language School are to ensure that each student will:

  • Develop the ability to fluently speak, listen, read, and write Vietnamese.
  • Develop the knowledge of Vietnamese history and culture.
  • Become responsible citizenS through sharing and volunteering work.

We are open every Sunday from 1:30pm to 4pm at Mariner High School location. We have been using "ToMa Thien" library for our school, please visit and explore more story books and music for your kids here.
